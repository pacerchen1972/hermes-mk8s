Nota informativa del CESID sobre incidentes durante las sesiones de la vista oral de la Causa 2/81 del Consejo Supremo de Justicia Militar. Miembros de las comisiones militares presentes en la sala participaron en aplausos, abucheos e increpaciones dirigidas a periodistas. El documento advierte de la previsible repetición de estos incidentes y sugiere medidas para prevenirlos.
EN

Military commission members present in the courtroom participated in applause, booing and heckling directed at journalists. The document warns of likely recurrence and suggests preventive measures.

The incidents that occurred yesterday in the courtroom during the hearing of case 2/81 of the C.S.J.M. included the participation of some members of the attending military commissions, as in the applause and boos as well as in the rebukes. nes aimed at journalists. Given the foreseeable occurrence of new incidents, provo fallen due to actions of the defendants or their defenders, It is considered advisable that the military representatives most characterized by each agency of the Cen Administration tral, Region or Zone are held responsible for the behavior of the members of the respective commission. On the other hand, it is estimated that the prolonged permanence of members in each commission can produce a distortion of attitudes, favorable to intervention in incidents of this class.
