Resumen / Summary

ES
Nota informativa del 28 de abril de 1982 sobre un incidente ocurrido durante la intervención del codefensor del General Milans del Bosch, el Teniente General Cabeza Calahorra. Al aludir a la prensa, esta se consideró insultada. La periodista Pilar Urbano (ABC) intentó hacer un desplante arrastrando a otros periodistas. Se intentó redactar un escrito al Presidente de la Sala para impedir nuevos insultos.
EN
Intelligence note of 28 April 1982 on an incident during the intervention of General Milans del Bosch's co-counsel, Lieutenant General Cabeza Calahorra. When he alluded to the press, journalists took offence. Journalist Pilar Urbano (ABC) attempted a walkout trying to bring other journalists with her. A written complaint to the court president was drafted to prevent further insults.
